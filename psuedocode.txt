Prompt user2 to guess a letter, show them the number of letters in the word, and limit the number of guesses they get

Check user2's guess against letters in secretWord:
  * Correct guess: Do not deduct a guess and print results with the correct letter in the correct position
  * Incorrect guess: Deduct 1 guess and print results

Repeat until the user guess the word or runs of out guesses:
  * Wins: Game over, You win
  * Loses: Game over You lose
